Our services

Our services are affordable and professional. We offer a wide range of services including:

  • Ultrasound

    • We have antenatol clinc and the MOH recommends for at lest 2 scans during the pregnancy period. This has helped us to manage our ANC Mothers better and give fast and accurate management incase of an complication.

  • Homebase Care

    • With covid 19 came a dire need and opportuanity for patient to be managed at home. home care also make it convinient for us to reach patients with chronic ailments and thode with needing palliatve care

  • Telemedicine

    • Our ultrasound, a product of Ilara Health, uses a technology where any healthcare provider can perform the ultrasound under the guidence of a lifted sonographer who the generates a detailed report for the patient. This has proved to be very effective because it eliminates the need for a sonographer at the facility and also patients can be attende to anywhere as the device is mobile and easily portable.

  • E-clinic Support Group

    • We have aninteractive fast growing online platform where we educate the the community about diseases, health education with enphasis on prevention and wellness checkups so as to detect disease such as HTN/DM and cancer in early stages.

  • Family Clinic

    • This department includes well baby visits and immunization, family planing, antinatol and postnatol care. Our trained, qualified licienced staff take care of mothers and babies offering holistic care from immunization, growth monitoring, nutritional assesment and advice as well walking the journey with the mothers who need support and encouragement.
