Services

outpatient services

  • Consultant service

    • Our hospital offers expert consultant services across various medical specialties, ensuring personalized care and treatment plans tailored to each patient's needs

  • Outpatient service

    • Patients can access medical care without being admitted to the hospital, benefiting from a range of diagnostic, therapeutic, and preventive services on an outpatient basis.

  • Labaratory service

    • Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with advanced technology to conduct a wide array of diagnostic tests, providing accurate and timely results to aid in patient care.

  • Telemedicine

    • We offer remote medical consultations and follow-ups via telemedicine, enabling patients to connect with healthcare providers conveniently from their homes or other locations.

  • Orthopedic services

    • Our orthopedic department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of musculoskeletal conditions, including fractures, joint disorders, and sports injuries.

  • Physiotherapy

    • Physiotherapy, also known as physical therapy, is a healthcare profession focused on helping individuals regain and maintain movement and function. It is a holistic approach that addresses a wide range of conditions affecting the musculoskeletal, neurological, and cardiopulmonary systems.

  • ENT(Ear, Nose, and Throat) Specialist

    • Our experienced ENT specialists provide comprehensive care for conditions affecting the ear, nose, throat, head, and neck, ranging from common ailments to complex surgical interventions.

  • Ultrasound

    • We offer ultrasound imaging services to aid in the diagnosis and monitoring of various medical conditions, providing non-invasive and real-time imaging for accurate assessment.

  • V.C.T (Voluntary Counseling and Testing)

    • Our hospital provides confidential counseling and testing services for HIV/AIDS and other sexually transmitted infections, empowering individuals to make informed decisions about their health.

  • Family planning

    • We offer comprehensive family planning services, including counseling, contraceptive options, and reproductive health education, to support individuals and couples in making informed choices about their family size and spacing.

  • Antenatal/Postnatal

    • Our dedicated maternal health services provide prenatal care, childbirth support, and postnatal care to ensure the health and well-being of both mother and baby throughout the pregnancy and after delivery.

  • Immunization

    • We offer a full range of immunizations for children and adults, following national guidelines to protect against preventable diseases and promote community health.

  • Home Based Care

    • For patients who require medical care at home, our hospital offers home-based care services, including skilled nursing, rehabilitation, and palliative care, delivered by trained healthcare professionals in the comfort of the patient's own environment.

  • Minor surgeries

    • Our facility is equipped to perform minor surgical procedures in a safe and sterile environment, ensuring optimal outcomes for patients requiring surgical intervention for various medical conditions
